Entering the Shifting Altars of Uznair

Should a portal appear when opening a treasure coffer found using a gazelleskin treasure map, it will lead to either the Lost Canals of Uznair or the Shifting Altars of Uznair. The destination will be chosen at random.

Level Requirement Disciple of War or Magic level 70
Item Level Requirement -
Item Level Sync 370
Party Size Eight players
Time Limit 60 minutes

Progressing through the Shifting Altars of Uznair

Upon entering the instance, players will find an arcane sphere which must be used to perform an invocation. Doing so will trigger a roulette wheel, summoning forth one of several creatures.

A treasure chest will appear upon defeating the enemies summoned by the invocation. Only the player who opened the teleportation portal can access it. After claiming the contents of the treasure chest, the arcane sphere can be used again, for a total of five invocations.

Leaving the Shifting Altars of Uznair

The duty will end if any of the following conditions are met:

Housing

Furnishings from the FFXIV Furnishing Design Contest have been added.

Learn more about the Furnishing Design Contest.

New furnishings have been added.

Mannequin furnishings are now available for estates and apartments.

Mannequins are interior furnishings that you can equip with clothing items. You may select their class or job, as well as gender. Various poses are also available. The mannequin can be used either to display an outfit for sale, or simply as decoration.

* As of Patch 4.4, only Miqo'te mannequins may be purchased and placed in your estate. Mannequins representing other races will be added in future patches.

Purchasing a Mannequin

  • You can purchase mannequins from the following NPCs:
    • Housing merchant in the Mist (X:10.8 Y:11.5)
    • Housing merchant in the Lavender Beds (X:11.8 Y:8.4)
    • Housing merchant in the Goblet (X:11.4 Y:9.4)
    • Housing merchant in Shirogane (X:10.0 Y:11.8)
    • Apartment merchants in apartment lobbies
    • Estate maidservants and manservants

Placing a Mannequin

  • To Place
    From the Housing menu, select Indoor Furnishings, followed by the mannequin you wish to place.
    You must also select a retainer to serve as merchant before you can place the mannequin.
  • Retainer Serving as Merchant
    One retainer may serve as merchant for multiple mannequins.
    To assign another retainer as merchant to a particular mannequin, you must remove it from you estate. You may assign another retainer when you place the mannequin again.

  • Limit per Estate

    Estate Type Mannequins
    Private Chamber / Apartment 4
    Cottage 6
    House 8
    Mansion 10

Selling Items via Mannequins

  • How to Sell
    Access the mannequin and choose "Select gear to sell," which opens the Mannequin Shop Settings window.
    Only sellable items in your inventory, Armoury Chest, or retainer inventory may be selected.

    Choose the equipment type (head, body, hands, etc.), followed by the item. You may then set the sale price.
    * Even if you place the mannequin in your storeroom or remove it from your estate while items are equipped, those items will remain equipped and may be bought/sold on the market.
  • Selling as a Set
    By checking "Sell Only as Set," you can sell all items equipped to the mannequin together. While the items may be viewed on the market board, a player must access the mannequin directly if they wish to purchase the set.

    When viewing items listed on the market board as part of a set, it is possible to send a tell to the seller to ascertain the location of their estate.
    * Please be aware that it is possible to view the name of a character selling a set through the market board.
  • After Items Are Sold
    Once items have been sold, "SOLD OUT" will be displayed for that equipment type, but the mannequin's appearance will not change until you select that equipment type again.
    Gil earned from sales of equipment will be held by your retainer.

    Listing Items for Sale
    Any items equipped on a mannequin that are listed for sale still occupy slots in the retainer's list. For that reason, a mannequin cannot be equipped with items that would cause the retainer to exceed the 20 item limit.

    • Displaying Gear

    You may also display items on a mannequin without listing the gear for sale. It is also possible to select unsellable items for display.
    * The mannequin cannot be used to display glamoured items.

    However, gear that is displayed also occupies slots in the retainer's list of items for sale.
    * An upcoming patch will allow you to display gear without occupying retainer sale slots.

    Purchasing Gear

    Accessing a mannequin will allow you to purchase the items that are currently for sale.

    Purchasing items via the market board incurs additional fees, whereas purchasing from a mannequin does not.
